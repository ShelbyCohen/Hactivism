<p><b>Lightstorm Entertainment</b> is an American <a href="/wiki/Film" title="Film">film</a> <a href="/wiki/Production_company" title="Production company">production company</a>. The company was founded by Canadian filmmaker <a href="/wiki/James_Cameron" title="James Cameron">James Cameron</a> and film producer Larry Kasanoff in 1990<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> and is best known for producing the films <i><a href="/wiki/Terminator_2:_Judgment_Day" title="Terminator 2: Judgment Day">Terminator 2: Judgment Day</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/Titanic_(1997_film)" title="Titanic (1997 film)">Titanic</a></i> and Cameron's latest film <i><a href="/wiki/Avatar_(2009_film)" title="Avatar (2009 film)">Avatar</a></i>. Cameron has also employed other filmmakers to produce and direct films under Lightstorm's production banner.</p>
