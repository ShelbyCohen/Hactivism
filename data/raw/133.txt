            <div class="thumb-wrap"><a  class="td-featured-img" href="http://www.showbiz411.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/09/States_california-hollywood-sign-E.jpeg" rel="bookmark" title="Mariah Carey&#8217;s Broken Engagement to James Packer Brings Divorce for Hollywood Financiers"><img width="686" height="352" itemprop="image" class="entry-thumb" src="http://www.showbiz411.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/09/States_california-hollywood-sign-E-686x352.jpeg" alt="" title="Mariah Carey&#8217;s Broken Engagement to James Packer Brings Divorce for Hollywood Financiers"/></a></div><input class="jpibfi" type="hidden"><p>This should be a lesson to everyone out there: don&#8217;t mix business and pleasure.</p>
<p>Director-producer Brett Ratner probably thought it was a great idea when he introduced his business partner, James Packer, to his great friend Mariah Carey. A romance developed, and the superstar singer became engaged to the billionaire sometime Scientologist from Australia.</p>
<p>Ratner and Packer, along with Steve Mnuchin, had formed a company called RatPac. They were financing most of Warner Bros.&#8217; film slate and having a lot of success. But no one probably thought, what if the love affair between Mariah and Packer goes south?</p>
<p>Well, it did. And today it was announced that Rat and Pac have divorced. Packer&#8217;s part of RatPac has been bought by Russian billionaire Len Blavatnik&#8217;s Access Industries. Blavatnik already owns Warner Music. Now he&#8217;s financing Warner Bros. studio movies. (The two Warners are separate companies.)</p>
<p>Mnuchin, who helped get Donald Trump elected president, is already out. He&#8217;s Trump&#8217;s Secretary of the Treasury. Now Packer is out, too, which is probably just as well. Packer was long associated with Scientology. Then with Ratner and Carey, it was thought he was out.</p>
<p>But you&#8217;re never &#8220;out&#8221; with Scientology if you have money. Last year he hired former Scientology celebrity wrangler (and still member of the cult) Tommy Davis to be his right hand man. This was a signal that the cult wanted Packer back in the fold. Who knows? Maybe now that he&#8217;s apart from mainstream Hollywood he&#8217;ll start funding <a href="http://www.showbiz411.com/2017/03/05/exclusive-tom-cruises-scientology-spy-now-movie-producer-gets-a-surprise-at-la-italia-film-fest">Scientology&#8217;s movies</a>.
