					<p>
	<span class="caption-box pull-left" style="float: left; width: 620px;"><img alt="On Monday, PAS’s defeated professional faction launched the new movement called GHB which they plan to later register as a political party. ― File pic" height="394" src="http://www.themalaymailonline.com/uploads/articles/2015-07/sabu1307.JPG" style=" float: left;" width="620"><span class="img-caption">On Monday, PAS’s defeated professional faction launched the new movement called GHB which they plan to later register as a political party. ― File pic</span></span>KUALA LUMPUR, July 16 ― A Muslim online group has cast doubt on PAS leader Dr Mohd Zuhdi Marzuki’s attack on the Gerakan Harapan Baru (GHB) splinter movement, questioning his sincerity in accusing the latter group of straying from Islam.</p>
<p>
	The Internet-based group called Suara Muslim Merdeka (SMM) also chastised the clergy class in PAS for its alleged “aggressive strategy” of slander and name-calling, which it said has turned the Islamist party into an exclusive “club” for those who pander to the clerics.</p>
<p>
	“What was he actually trying spin by issuing such statements? Was it to show that GHB is not Islamic, or was it just to criticise GHB for not fitting to the demands and reasonings of the ustaz gang, of which Zuhdi is one?” asked the group’s representative Azzam Ahmad in a statement.</p>
<p>
	On Tuesday, PAS central working committee member Zuhdi accused GHB members of cosying up to so-called “liberals” such as women rights group Sisters in Islam (SIS), civil society Lawyers for Liberty, and the lesbian, gay, bisexual and transgenders (LGBT).</p>
<p>
	Zuhdi also claimed that GHB has strayed from Islamic teachings by adopting a stance similar to Indonesian group Jaringan Islam Liberal and the American strain of progressive Islam.</p>
<p>
	But SMM explained that GHB members’ involvement with the groups named by Zuhdi has only been limited to dialogues and discourse to correct their perception of Islam.</p>
<p>
	“What has Zuhdi’s friends, this ustaz gang, done besides labeling GHB? Has it debated with SIS activists?” asked Azzam.</p>
<p>
	“Have they given free legal aid to wives and children who were abandoned, to break the support base of SIS that it has built through its charity work?”</p>
<p>
	The group also criticised the contribution of several PAS leaders to the party in terms of ideas and policies, especially former Youth chief Nasruddin Hassan; president Datuk Seri Abdul Hadi Awang’s son-in-law, Zaharuddin Muhammad; and Youth chief Nik Mohamad Abduh Nik Abd Aziz.</p>
<p>
	On Monday, PAS’s defeated professional faction launched the new movement called GHB which they plan to later register as a political party, and will go on a nationwide tour from now until August to convey to the public its goals.</p>
<p>
	Selangor PAS has since sacked all its members who are involved with the movement, despite the federal PAS saying that no action will be taken as long as there are no complaints lodged against them.</p>
<p>
	PAS Ulama Wing has also warned party members today against GHB’s “satanic” invitation to leave the party, claiming a new party formed by GHB will solely be fueled by its members’ “resentment”.</p>
