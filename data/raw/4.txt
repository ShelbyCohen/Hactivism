<div class="crew_slider">
  <h1>Meet the Crew!</h1>
	<ul class="slides">
		
         <li class="slide">
<div class="crew-image"><img width="208" height="308" src="http://www.pacerkidsagainstbullying.org/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/crew-brad.jpg" class="attachment-full" alt="" style="" title="" /></div>
<div class="crew-audio"><!--[if lt IE 9]><script>document.createElement('audio');</script><![endif]-->
<audio class="wp-audio-shortcode" id="audio-183-1" preload="none" style="width: 100%; visibility: hidden;" controls="controls"><source type="audio/mpeg" src="http://www.pacerkidsagainstbullying.org/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/BRAD-BIO.mp3?_=1" /><a href="http://www.pacerkidsagainstbullying.org/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/BRAD-BIO.mp3">http://www.pacerkidsagainstbullying.org/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/BRAD-BIO.mp3</a></audio></div>
<div class="crew-body"><p>Hey, you. Yeah, you. I’m Brad. Rhymes with bad. You know what my favorite thing is? Making babies like you cry. Don’t even think about telling the teacher I said that. If you do, I’ll give you something to really cry about.</p>
</div>
</li>

		
         <li class="slide">
<div class="crew-image"><img width="208" height="308" src="http://www.pacerkidsagainstbullying.org/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/crew-carmen.jpg" class="attachment-full" alt="" style="" title="" /></div>
<div class="crew-audio"><audio class="wp-audio-shortcode" id="audio-196-2" preload="none" style="width: 100%; visibility: hidden;" controls="controls"><source type="audio/mpeg" src="http://www.pacerkidsagainstbullying.org/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/CARMEN-BIO.mp3?_=2" /><a href="http://www.pacerkidsagainstbullying.org/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/CARMEN-BIO.mp3">http://www.pacerkidsagainstbullying.org/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/CARMEN-BIO.mp3</a></audio></div>
<div class="crew-body"><p>¡Hola! I’m Carmen! Not many people know this, but I have epilepsy. The signals in my brain get mixed up. Sometimes I space out. I take medication so it hardly ever happens anymore. But once it happened at school. Some kids made fun of me. I felt so bad. I don’t want anyone to feel that way.</p>
<p>When I see kids picking on other kids, it makes me so mad! Usually I just grab some friends and we march right over and say, “stop it!” Together we can stop bullying.</p>
</div>
</li>

		
         <li class="slide">
<div class="crew-image"><img width="208" height="308" src="http://www.pacerkidsagainstbullying.org/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/crew-chandra.jpg" class="attachment-full" alt="" style="" title="" /></div>
<div class="crew-audio"><audio class="wp-audio-shortcode" id="audio-197-3" preload="none" style="width: 100%; visibility: hidden;" controls="controls"><source type="audio/mpeg" src="http://www.pacerkidsagainstbullying.org/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/CHANDRA-BIO.mp3?_=3" /><a href="http://www.pacerkidsagainstbullying.org/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/CHANDRA-BIO.mp3">http://www.pacerkidsagainstbullying.org/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/CHANDRA-BIO.mp3</a></audio></div>
<div class="crew-body"><p>Hi. I’m Chandra.</p>
<p>I just moved here with my mom and dad and little brother.  He has autism. I don’t really know many people yet. But I see a lot of things going on. Like kids being picked on because they’re different.</p>
<p>It’s just wrong. I try to stop it&#8211;even if I feel afraid. Two things I know for sure: Nobody likes bullying, and everyone deserves respect.</p>
</div>
</li>

		
         <li class="slide">
<div class="crew-image"><img width="208" height="308" src="http://www.pacerkidsagainstbullying.org/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/crew-jay.jpg" class="attachment-full" alt="" style="" title="" /></div>
<div class="crew-audio"><audio class="wp-audio-shortcode" id="audio-795-4" preload="none" style="width: 100%; visibility: hidden;" controls="controls"><source type="audio/mpeg" src="http://www.pacerkidsagainstbullying.org/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/JAY-BIO.mp3?_=4" /><a href="http://www.pacerkidsagainstbullying.org/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/JAY-BIO.mp3">http://www.pacerkidsagainstbullying.org/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/JAY-BIO.mp3</a></audio></div>
<div class="crew-body"><p>Hi, I’m Jay.</p>
<p>Yep, I use sign language. That’s because I’m hard of hearing. My hearing aids help a little, but I don’t hear like most people. When I talk, people have trouble understanding me sometimes. But you know what’s cool? I can read lips! It’s, It&#8217;s like being a super spy!</p>
<p>Maybe you could come over sometime. We could watch TV! I could show you how closed captioning helps me understand what they’re saying.</p>
</div>
</li>

		
         <li class="slide">
<div class="crew-image"><img width="208" height="308" src="http://www.pacerkidsagainstbullying.org/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/crew-matt.jpg" class="attachment-full" alt="" style="" title="" /></div>
<div class="crew-audio"><audio class="wp-audio-shortcode" id="audio-796-5" preload="none" style="width: 100%; visibility: hidden;" controls="controls"><source type="audio/mpeg" src="http://www.pacerkidsagainstbullying.org/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/MATT-BIO.mp3?_=5" /><a href="http://www.pacerkidsagainstbullying.org/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/MATT-BIO.mp3">http://www.pacerkidsagainstbullying.org/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/MATT-BIO.mp3</a></audio></div>
<div class="crew-body"><p>I’m Matt. You got something to say?</p>
<p>Hey, I’m better at sports than you’ll ever be. Hit a grand slam in Little League last year. I scored the winning run! Bet you couldn’t do that in a million years. I’ll probably do it again this year, if I feel like it. Yeah, I’m the best hitter on the team. On any team. Better’n you, that’s for sure.</p>
</div>
</li>

		
         <li class="slide">
<div class="crew-image"><img width="208" height="308" src="http://www.pacerkidsagainstbullying.org/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/crew-mickey.jpg" class="attachment-full" alt="" style="" title="" /></div>
<div class="crew-audio"><audio class="wp-audio-shortcode" id="audio-797-6" preload="none" style="width: 100%; visibility: hidden;" controls="controls"><source type="audio/mpeg" src="http://www.pacerkidsagainstbullying.org/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/MICKEY-BIO.mp3?_=6" /><a href="http://www.pacerkidsagainstbullying.org/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/MICKEY-BIO.mp3">http://www.pacerkidsagainstbullying.org/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/MICKEY-BIO.mp3</a></audio></div>
<div class="crew-body"><p>Hi! I&#8217;m Mickey. I have Down syndrome. Sometimes when I talk, you might not understand me. But I usually understand you. In school, I am in special ed classes.</p>
<p>I LOVE baseball. I even won a medal! Cool!</p>
</div>
</li>

		
         <li class="slide">
<div class="crew-image"><img width="208" height="308" src="http://www.pacerkidsagainstbullying.org/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/crew-molly.jpg" class="attachment-full" alt="" style="" title="" /></div>
<div class="crew-audio"><audio class="wp-audio-shortcode" id="audio-798-7" preload="none" style="width: 100%; visibility: hidden;" controls="controls"><source type="audio/mpeg" src="http://www.pacerkidsagainstbullying.org/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/MOLLY-BIO.mp3?_=7" /><a href="http://www.pacerkidsagainstbullying.org/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/MOLLY-BIO.mp3">http://www.pacerkidsagainstbullying.org/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/MOLLY-BIO.mp3</a></audio></div>
<div class="crew-body"><p>My name is Molly! Want to play with me at recess?</p>
<p>I like it when kids have fun together. Sometimes I see kids being picked on, and I feel scared. I want to make it stop, but I don’t know what to do. Do you ever feel that way? No one has picked on me yet.  I don’t know what I would do if they did. Usually I try to hang around with my friends, ‘cause together we’re amazing!</p>
</div>
</li>

		
         <li class="slide">
<div class="crew-image"><img width="208" height="308" src="http://www.pacerkidsagainstbullying.org/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/crew-mrsbridge.jpg" class="attachment-full" alt="" style="" title="" /></div>
<div class="crew-audio"><audio class="wp-audio-shortcode" id="audio-799-8" preload="none" style="width: 100%; visibility: hidden;" controls="controls"><source type="audio/mpeg" src="http://www.pacerkidsagainstbullying.org/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/MRS-BRIDGE-BIO.mp3?_=8" /><a href="http://www.pacerkidsagainstbullying.org/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/MRS-BRIDGE-BIO.mp3">http://www.pacerkidsagainstbullying.org/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/MRS-BRIDGE-BIO.mp3</a></audio></div>
<div class="crew-body"><p>Hi! I’m Mrs. Bridge. I’m so glad you’re here! I hope you have fun and learn a lot about how to stop bullying.</p>
<p>I have a little secret.  When I was your age, kids picked on me. It’s true! That’s because I had a disability called ADHD. That’s short for “attention deficit hyperactivity disorder.” I even landed in trouble with my teachers. And now I am a teacher!</p>
</div>
</li>

		
         <li class="slide">
<div class="crew-image"><img width="208" height="308" src="http://www.pacerkidsagainstbullying.org/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/crew-nick.jpg" class="attachment-full" alt="" style="" title="" /></div>
<div class="crew-audio"><audio class="wp-audio-shortcode" id="audio-800-9" preload="none" style="width: 100%; visibility: hidden;" controls="controls"><source type="audio/mpeg" src="http://www.pacerkidsagainstbullying.org/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/NICK-BIO.mp3?_=9" /><a href="http://www.pacerkidsagainstbullying.org/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/NICK-BIO.mp3">http://www.pacerkidsagainstbullying.org/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/NICK-BIO.mp3</a></audio></div>
<div class="crew-body"><p>Hi, I’m Nick.</p>
<p>Am I the only one who isn’t sure what to do when someone’s being bullied?</p>
<p>I wish people could just get along like me and my friends. Sometimes I see a kid being picked on and I don’t know what to do. I feel really bad. Should I tell? Should I try to stop it? If I do, will the kids doing it come after me? Or will I be blamed? I want to do something, but sometimes I just feel helpless.</p>
</div>
</li>

		
         <li class="slide">
<div class="crew-image"><img width="208" height="308" src="http://www.pacerkidsagainstbullying.org/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/crew-pip.jpg" class="attachment-full" alt="" style="" title="" /></div>
<div class="crew-audio"><audio class="wp-audio-shortcode" id="audio-801-10" preload="none" style="width: 100%; visibility: hidden;" controls="controls"><source type="audio/mpeg" src="http://www.pacerkidsagainstbullying.org/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/PIP-BIO.mp3?_=10" /><a href="http://www.pacerkidsagainstbullying.org/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/PIP-BIO.mp3">http://www.pacerkidsagainstbullying.org/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/PIP-BIO.mp3</a></audio></div>
<div class="crew-body"><p>My name is Pipsqueak. You can call me Pip!</p>
<p>Before I came here, I lived in a pet shop with about a gazillion other hamsters. I was the littlest one.  Boy, did the other hamsters let me know it! They teased me and called me names. Hamsters can be so mean! That’s where I learned something about bullies: They just want you to cry and feel bad so they can feel powerful. Know what I did when I was picked on? I just twitched my nose and said, “Pifflesticks!”</p>
</div>
</li>

		
         <li class="slide">
<div class="crew-image"><img width="208" height="308" src="http://www.pacerkidsagainstbullying.org/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/crew-sally1.jpg" class="attachment-full" alt="" style="" title="" /></div>
<div class="crew-audio"><audio class="wp-audio-shortcode" id="audio-48-11" preload="none" style="width: 100%; visibility: hidden;" controls="controls"><source type="audio/mpeg" src="http://www.pacerkidsagainstbullying.org/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/SALLY-BIO.mp3?_=11" /><a href="http://www.pacerkidsagainstbullying.org/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/SALLY-BIO.mp3">http://www.pacerkidsagainstbullying.org/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/SALLY-BIO.mp3</a></audio></div>
<div class="crew-body"><p>Hi, I&#8217;m Sally! I have cerebral palsy. My muscles don’t always do what I want them to do. But I have this cool red wheelchair. I can go just about anywhere and do almost anything. Last summer I even went to camp and went horseback riding! It was so fun.</p>
<p>Do you like computers? I do. A lot! You should see me surf the Web and play adventure games. I’m awesome!</p>
</div>
</li>

		
         <li class="slide">
<div class="crew-image"><img width="208" height="308" src="http://www.pacerkidsagainstbullying.org/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/crew-shannon.jpg" class="attachment-full" alt="" style="" title="" /></div>
<div class="crew-audio"><audio class="wp-audio-shortcode" id="audio-802-12" preload="none" style="width: 100%; visibility: hidden;" controls="controls"><source type="audio/mpeg" src="http://www.pacerkidsagainstbullying.org/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/SHANNON-BIO.mp3?_=12" /><a href="http://www.pacerkidsagainstbullying.org/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/SHANNON-BIO.mp3">http://www.pacerkidsagainstbullying.org/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/SHANNON-BIO.mp3</a></audio></div>
<div class="crew-body"><p>I’m Shannon—but I’m sure you already know that. Don’t you think I’m, like, the coolest girl in school? I do. Do you like my new cell phone? Maybe I’ll use it to take a picture of that new girl. I could post it on the Internet with a message about what a loser she is.</p>
<p>Good thing my older sister, Marla, and her friends haven’t thought of doing that to me. They are, like, so mean! Marla even told one of my friends that I still suck my thumb at night. I do not!</p>
</div>
</li>

		
</ul>
